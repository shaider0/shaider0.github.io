About Me

Software Developer | UX Enthusiast | Experienced Biotech Project Manager

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website! To tell you a little about myself, I spent 8 years in various managerial roles in the biotechnology and pharmaceutical industry, most recently as a Senior Manager at Alexion Pharmaceuticals. Throughout my career, I've had the privelege of working with some amazing colleagues to help run clinical trials for some truly ground-breaking medical therapies.

In 2018, I started teaching myself to program in my free time using online resources such as Codecademy, Udemy, and Udacity. I quickly found that I really enjoyed programming and wanted to expand my skillset. In early 2019, I decided to enroll in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive program, a 12-week 500+ hour program with classes taking place daily at GA's Boston Campus. At GA, through daily lessons, coding challenges, and 4 full-stack projects, I was able to learn more about software development than I thought was possible in such a short period of time.

After graduating from GA in June of 2019, before jumping back into the job market, I decided to take some time to travel around the world with my wife. We were lucky enough to visit some truly amazing cities including Paris, Berlin, Rome, Bali, and Dhaka. Now I'm back, recharged, and ready to get to work!

Below, you'll find descriptions, screenshots, and links to code repositories for some of the software applications I've built. The majority of the applications were built in 2019 and started during my time at GA. The featured project, the one I'm most proud of, was started late in 2019 while I was traveling throughout Europe, with finishing touches applied in April 2020. I hope these projects will demonstrate some of my skills as a developer. Enjoy!
