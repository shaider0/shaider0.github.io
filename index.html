About Me

Software Developer | UX Enthusiast

Thanks for visiting my portfolio website! To tell you a little about myself, I spent 8 years in various managerial roles in the pharmaceutical industry. Over the years, I had the privilege of working with some amazing colleagues to help run clinical trials for some truly ground-breaking medical therapies.

In 2018, I started teaching myself to code in my free time and quickly fell in love. In early 2019, I enrolled in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive Program. Through daily lessons, coding challenges, and 3 full-stack projects, I was able to learn more about software development than I thought possible in such a short period of time.

After graduating from GA (and nearly 10 years without a break), I decided to take some time off to travel with my wife. We were lucky enough to visit some truly amazing cities including Paris, Berlin, & Rome. While traveling around Europe, I snuck a spare moment here and there to chip away at a prototype for what would eventually become JoyList, an app that I created with a software development partner (more on that below in the experience section). While founding JoyList has taught me a ton about what it takes to create and launch a software product from end to end, it also made me realize how much I enjoy (and miss) coding. I've decided now to look for my first full-time software developer role so I can get back to doing what what I enjoy most - building cool stuff and solving problems.

Below, you'll find previews of some of the applications I've built to showcase what I'm capable of as a developer. If you want to know more about me, check out my blog. Thanks for reading!
